How It Works

1. Select Your Ride

At Pedal Pass, we offer three types of eco-friendly transportation options to suit your needs:

  • E-Scooter: Perfect for zipping through the city with ease.
  • E-Bike: Ideal for longer, more comfortable rides with pedal-assist.
  • Standard Bike: A classic, simple, and eco-friendly way to get around.
2. Payment

Pay online for your ride, securely and hassle-free. You’ll only pay for the time you use, by the minute.

3. Unlock & Ride

Once your payment is confirmed, you can head to your selected bike or scooter and unlock it. The website will guide you through the process of unlocking and starting your ride.

4. Return Your Ride

When you’re finished, simply return your bike or scooter to one of our designated drop-off locations around the city. Lock it securely, and your ride will automatically end online. You’ll be billed based on the total time you spent riding.
